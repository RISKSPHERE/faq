Location Rating Improvement Guide

Rating 5

Q. How can I update the name of the building?

+

A1. You can click on the “Edit Building Name” Button and provide the updated building name. In this case Geo-coding will not be executed again.

Q. How can I add more building or sub destinations in the vicinity of the Location or Address?

+

A1. The Location has a rating of 5 as the Location pin is found on the rooftop. However, it seems that there are more buildings associated with that location in the vicinity. To include these locations in the Location List (also referred as SOV), you can click on the “Add sub destination" Button situated at the top right corner of the Map.

It will show all additional Building with blue markers and original building with the red marker. You can right click on these Blue Pin Markers or click from the list below the map and click on “Add to SOV” to add the location in the location list or SOV.

Rating 4

Q. How can I improve the score of the location?

+

A1. You can click on “Edit Location”, to update the Building Name. In this case the application will rerun the geo-coding with the updated building name and re-calculate the Rating of the location.

A2. You can search your building from the search box and select your property; after selecting the property from the list, you can confirm your building. After the confirmation, the score will be updated to 5. and in case of not confirming, you can retry for improvement of score.
